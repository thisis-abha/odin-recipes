Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled Cheese Sandwich is kinda cool and low effort

Shitty Grilled Cheese is easy to make. Though, making a good one takes your soul.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Some kind of cheese
  • Oregano?

Steps

  1. Heat some butter on a pan
  2. Place the bread on the pan
  3. Add whatever cheese on the bread piece, add all kinds of cheese you have
  4. Put a bread piece on the cheese, which is infact on the bread piece we put earlier. This recipe has layerssss.
  5. If greedy or depressed, repeat steps 3 and 4 to make it a 3 layer Sandwich
  6. Hopefully, our first bread piece isn't burnt,flip the sandwich upside down
  7. At this stage, you either have a decent looking sandwich, or you are in a total mess. If its the latter, start with step one again
  8. Place it on a plate
  9. Eat
